About Temp's Menu

Temp's Menu is a free mod menu for Gorilla Tag, created as an homage to the popular 2023 Oddie's Client. Now developed by Temp (formerly known as Oddie), this menu brings enhanced gameplay features and customization options to your Gorilla Tag experience.

This mod menu provides a variety of modifications to enhance your gameplay, from movement enhancements to visual improvements. All features are designed to work seamlessly with Gorilla Tag.
